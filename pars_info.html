Parmeter info

prefixes m = parameter value; v = parameter variance
lam Exponential growth parameter. Only the values applicable since the start of the predictions are used.
icp Proportion of hospitalized patients that will require IC
adp Among patients in need of intensive care, proportion who will be admitted. Normally equal to 1 without variance.
lag Time lag between hospital admission and ICU transfer
los Length of stay in ICU

The parameter table can be edited. Right click to add or delete a line.
